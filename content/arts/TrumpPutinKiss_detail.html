Trump & Putin Kusspuppen

Trump & Putin Kusspuppen, 2019
US$1,500
wood, acrylic, magnets
10" high x 3" wide x 1" deep

When you bring these dolls near each other, Trump can’t help himself and gives Putin a big, deep kiss.

Based on the Dutch Kusspupen (Kissing Puppet) figurines of the 1950’s, these updated kissing dolls play off Trump's claim that his political enemies are kissing buddies: “I could give you 100 pictures of him [ Robert Mueller ] and Comey hugging and kissing each other.”

They also draw inspiration from the famous Berlin Wall mural of East Germany's Erich Honecker and Soviet leader Leonid Brezhnev kissing as well as a more recent mural on a Lithuanian restaurant depicting Trump kissing Putin.

But wait, there’s more! Trump also comes with a special, secret Putin-pleasing action! Just bring Trump’s mouth near Putin’s crotch and get out of the way as Trump pleasures Putin! Now we know what really happens in their private off-the-record meetings!
