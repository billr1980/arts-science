Mad King Trump

Filet-O-Fish™, French Fries, Quarter Pounder with Cheese™, Chicken McNuggets™, Spicy BBQ Sauce™, onion, pickles, tomatoes, American cheese, particle board
14" wide x 24" high

Place your bets! Which will last longer - McDonalds lover Donald Trump , or a Donald Trump made from McDonalds food?

Inspired by the lettuce that outlasted Liz Truss's disastrous term as Prime Minister of England, artists around the world are invited to share their interpretation of the decay and rot currently infesting the United States government.

Artists are not restricted to portraying Trump - anyone enabling Trump's fascist acts is eligible, such as Elon Musk.

I am looking for art created with organic materials such as vegetables, flowers, seeds, fruit, fish, meat, cheese, and milk. Any organic material is allowed, as long as it will rot.

I want photographs of the artwork when it is first completed, and a series of photographs documenting its decay over days or weeks.

I am searching for an organization that can host an online exhibition of the submitted works. Please contact me at williamross . net (remove the spaces) to discuss.

I am hoping for a people-powered, international repudiation of the horrible creatures destroying the rule of law in the United States. A show of solidarity of the people of the world united against Trump, all the people who just want to get along and are appalled by Trump's shattering of long-standing friendly relations
