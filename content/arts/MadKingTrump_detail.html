A Reason for Hope: the Circle of Life Still Turns

Goal:
This project asks: "Which will last longer - McDonalds lover Trump, or a Trump made from McDonalds food?"
Results:
Results indicate the the Mcdonalds portrait will easily outlast its subject. Based on his rate of decline, Trump is projected to leave office due to failing health by December 5, 2025.
